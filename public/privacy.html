Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2025

APEX Career AI ("APEX", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

TL;DR: Your data stays yours. We store it in your personal Supabase account with row-level security. AI requests go directly from your browser to the AI provider using your own API key. We never see, store, or proxy your AI conversations.

1. Information We Collect

Account information:

  • Email address (used for authentication)
  • Password (hashed by Supabase Auth - we never see your plaintext password)

Data you provide:

  • Job descriptions, company names, role titles, and application status
  • Your master resume and tailored resume outputs
  • Interview post-mortem notes (what went well, struggles, questions asked, culture notes)
  • AI-generated analysis results (stored alongside your job data)
  • LinkedIn profile text (if you use the import feature - processed once, not stored separately)

API keys you provide:

  • Your AI provider API keys (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Perplexity) are stored in your profile row in the database
  • Keys are used only to make AI requests directly from your browser to the respective provider
  • We never proxy, log, or access your API keys server-side

OAuth data (if using Google or LinkedIn sign-in):

  • Basic profile information (name, email) provided by the OAuth provider
  • We do not request or store access to your Google Drive, Gmail, LinkedIn connections, or any other extended data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: To create and manage your account
  • Core features: To store your job tracking data, resumes, and post-mortems so they persist across sessions
  • AI features: Your data is sent directly from your browser to the AI provider you selected - it never passes through our servers

3. How AI Requests Work

When you use any AI feature (JD analysis, resume tailoring, match analysis, prep tips, LinkedIn import), the request travels directly from your browser to the AI provider's API using your API key. APEX acts as a frontend interface only. We do not operate a backend proxy, relay, or logging layer for AI calls.

This means:

  • Your resume content, job descriptions, and interview notes are sent to the AI provider you chose (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, or Perplexity)
  • These providers have their own privacy policies and data retention practices - please review them
  • We have no visibility into what you send or what the AI returns

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Database: All user data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Row Level Security (RLS) enabled. This means each user can only read and write their own data at the database level - even if someone obtained the database connection, they could not access another user's rows.
  • Authentication: Handled by Supabase Auth with industry-standard practices (bcrypt password hashing, JWT tokens, secure session management)
  • Transport: All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • API keys: Stored in your profile row. We recommend using keys with minimal permissions and rotating them periodically.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties. Period.

The only external services that receive your data are:

  • Supabase: Hosts our database and authentication (Supabase Privacy Policy)
  • AI providers: Only when you explicitly trigger an AI feature, and only the provider you selected
  • Vercel: Hosts the frontend application (Vercel Privacy Policy)

6. Data Retention

  • Your data is retained as long as your account exists
  • You can delete individual jobs, post-mortems, or your resume at any time
  • You can delete your entire account from Settings, which permanently removes all associated data
  • Deleted data is not recoverable

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored in your account (it's all visible in the app)
  • Modify any of your data at any time
  • Delete individual records or your entire account
  • Export your data (available through the application)
  • Withdraw consent by deleting your account

8. Cookies and Tracking

APEX uses only essential cookies required for authentication (Supabase session tokens). We do not use:

  • Analytics cookies
  • Advertising cookies or tracking pixels
  • Third-party tracking scripts
  • Fingerprinting or any behavioral tracking

9. Children's Privacy

APEX is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of APEX after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
